Stroop Test

Instructions:

A word will be displayed at the top in a certain colour. For example:

RED

Below this will be a grid of 9 buttons. You must click the one that SAYS the colour that the above word is COLOURED as.

For the example above, you would click BLUE

Here are some more examples to help you understand:

For YELLOW, click GREEN

For PINK, click RED

For BLUE, click WHITE

You may find this difficult, and that is okay: That's the whole point.

The test will gradually get more difficult the higher you score, starting at 3 buttons before eventually expanding to 9.

There is a timer, which also gradually goes down faster. You have 3 strikes before it's game over.

Once you have read and ensured that you comprehend these instructions, click the button below to begin.
